About Me

My name is Edwin Hadass. I am a student at the University of Warwick, currently studying Computer Science. This website is a place where you can find descriptions of and reflections on some of the programming projects and ideas that I have worked on in my spare time.

I have a lot of experience in object-oriented and low-level programming, especially with the C-family of languages, alongside Java, Haskell, Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have worked with a lot of game-development and graphical programming tools including but not limited to: Unity, Godot, Monogame, SDL, OpenGL, as well as web frameworks such as Flask.
